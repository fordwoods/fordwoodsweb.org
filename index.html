Our Mission

FordWoods Web Development grew out of a lifelong desire to give back to foster care. For more than 30 years, that mission stayed present—even when the right form for it did not yet exist. Over time, it became clear that the greatest barrier was not willingness or care, but access: access to learning, clarity, and tools that people could understand and keep.

FordWoods was created to meet that need. By building websites in real time and openly sharing what is learned along the way, this nonprofit supports stability, ownership, and continuity—values shaped by lived experience.

FordWoods exists so that no one is left behind simply because something felt too complicated to finish.

Community Support

Our Approach

Transparency

We work openly, sharing our process and decisions. You're not just getting a finished product—you're learning how it was built and why.

Ownership

What we build is truly yours. We provide clear documentation and transfer full control so you can maintain and grow your web presence independently.

Accessibility

We remove barriers. Our websites are designed to be understood, used, and maintained by people regardless of their technical background.

Continuity

We build for the long term. Our goal is to create digital tools that grow with you and remain useful for years to come.

Who We Serve

Individuals

People with lived experience in foster care who need a professional web presence for their work, advocacy, or personal projects. We help turn ideas into reality without the usual barriers.

Organizations

Nonprofits, advocacy groups, and community organizations serving the foster care community. We provide the digital infrastructure needed to expand reach and impact.
